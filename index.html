I am a research scientist at Isomorphic Labs.
Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at EPFL, as part of the LTS2 and LPDI laboratories.

I research graph neural networks and their applications to dynamical systems and computational biology. I am also the creator of Spektral, a library for graph deep learning in TensorFlow/Keras.

I obtained my PhD at the Swiss AI Lab IDSIA. I hold an MSc in Computer Science and Engineering with honors from Politecnico di Milano.

In my free time, I co-host and manage Smarter Podcast, a live-streaming podcast in Italian in which we interview AI researchers from academia and industry.
