I am an AI Scientist working on multimodality and post-training in large language models.

Previously, I was a Staff Research Scientist leading the Generative Models team at Isomorphic Labs, an Alphabet company on a mission to solve all disease with the power of AI.

Before that, I completed my PhD at the Swiss AI Lab IDSIA, where I researched graph neural networks and their applications to dynamical systems and biology. I then worked as a researcher at EPFL, focusing on neural fields and generative models for protein design.

I am also the creator of Spektral, the first open-source library for geometric deep learning in TensorFlow/Keras. Beyond research, I have hosted a podcast on AI in Italian, and I am deeply fascinated by self-organizing systems and emergence.
