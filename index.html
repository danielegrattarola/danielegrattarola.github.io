I am the Head of Machine Learning at Proxima, where we're expanding the frontier of drug discovery by making protein interactions programmable through generative AI.

Previously, I led the Generative Models team at Isomorphic Labs and I worked on large-scale synthetic data generation and multimodal LLMs at Mistral AI.

Before moving to industry, I completed my PhD at the Swiss AI Lab IDSIA, where I researched graph neural networks and their applications to dynamical systems and biology. I then worked as a researcher at EPFL, focusing on neural fields and generative models for protein design.

I am also the creator of Spektral, the first open-source library for geometric deep learning in TensorFlow/Keras. Beyond research, I have hosted a podcast on AI in Italian, I am deeply fascinated by self-organizing systems and emergence, and I enjoy cooking and hiking in the Alps.
